About Us

Wakio and Musa By Choice Funeral Services are well established private companies in Mombasa and Voi. Musa By Choice Started as a photography and video coverage company and later in 2015 merged with Wakio Funeral Services for efficient service delivery. Within the short time, both companies have been able to grow up powerfully, gathering reputation both Locally and Internationaly. This has led to extention of our vast variety of services.


We Offer Cremation, Repartition, Flowers, Headstone, Coffins, Body transfer, Lowering gear, Funeral programs, Banner and T-Shirt Designing. Musa By Choice also offers Video coverage and Photography (All occassions),video editing, wedding programmes, digital printing (photos), Public Address system, Hiring Tents and Chairs.

The company is a genuine full service family owned company whose main goal is to offer standard services regardless of race or religion or political stand
